A Legacy Built Together

For four decades at Chimulkar Financial Services, we’ve stood by our clients and their families, helping them plan, grow, and secure their futures. The Chimulkar Foundation is the natural next chapter: a foundation born out of 40 years of trust, designed to help them channel their success into meaningful impact.

Why The Foundation

True wealth is not only what we achieve, but what we pass on. Getting behind the idea of “Legacy Forward” offers investors a chance to extend their journey, transforming personal milestones into opportunities for others. By participating, you not only secure your own legacy, but help rewrite the stories of those who need it most.

The Mission that drives

Our mission is to ensure that once you’ve reached your goals, you have a meaningful way to give back. With transparency, structure, and accountability, we create a platform where giving is not charity, but legacy.
